Our Story

One Oven.
One Guy.
Every Friday.

Tooley spent years in corporate America before he realized the thing he actually wanted to build was on a cutting board, not in a conference room.

He traded spreadsheets for San Marzano tomatoes, sourced the best local ingredients he could find, fired up a Gozney pizza oven, and started showing up every Friday in Grant Park. No shortcuts. No reheats. Just two artisanal pies, made from scratch, every week.

DNTLVCRMBS isn’t a restaurant. It’s a ritual.
